Expand Spring Street Island!

Goal

Remove asphalt around the Spring street island and replace with green space. Possibly plant 3 new trees in the expanded space.

We could narrow the streets on all sides (no changes to traffic flow) or remove a street on one side of the island. Somerville can choose the best solution.

Benefits

  • Increase green space 3x
  • Traffic calming
  • More trees
  • Community space
  • Add pollinator habitat

Process

Ben Ewen-Campen created a council order for Mayor Ballantyne to consider this project.

We need to gather support for the Mayor to approve. If you'd like to help, contact Jeff O'Neill at volunteer@foospringstreetisland.com to learn more.

How to Help

Encourage people and organizations to support and be listed below.

Raise the issue with Somerville elected officials and other relevant people who work for the city.
